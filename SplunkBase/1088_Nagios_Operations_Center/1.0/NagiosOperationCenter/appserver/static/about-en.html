Summary

Nagios Operations Center, Executive y Capacity Planning: enhance your current Nagios deployment without changing a thing in your current configuration.

This application enhances the current operative capacity of your Nagios deployment, contributing with Executive, IT Operations, Capacity Planning, and Help Desk information to your IT monitoring infrastructure.

As all the business Operations views converge on these dashboards, and an improved IT-infrastructure Executive view is available, this app shows the state of your business critical assets and SLAs in Real-Time, as well as more technical and operational views.

Features

Infrastructure State

  • Current active CRITICAL alerts
  • Curent active WARNING alerts
  • Current active UNKNOWN alerts
  • Total active alerts (in chart)
  • Total active alerts (in table)
  • Real-Time log of received notifications

Alerts report

  • Counter of alerts by time, including CRITICAL, UNKNOWN and WARNING
  • Alerts, most happening (chart)
  • Alerts, most happening (table)
  • Alerts, by duration (chart)
  • Alerts, by duration (table)

Notifications Report

  • Top-10 of contacted persons
  • Top-10 of affected services
  • Timeline of sent notifications (chart)
  • Notifications, by type

Also in Premium version...

  • Servicegroups and Hostgroups timeline
  • SLA evolution chart Consolidated SLA
  • Average time (hours) spent in resolutions, by severity
  • Total count of schedule downtimes
  • Average and total time spent in schedule downtimes
